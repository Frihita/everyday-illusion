THE EVERYDAY ILLUSION

"White bread or brown bread — which is truly better for daily health?"

"Brown bread isn't always healthier. The color doesn't prove it's whole grain. Always check for '100% whole grain' or high fiber content in ingredients."

"Don't trust the color; trust the label."

"Maida versus whole wheat — is whole wheat always the smarter choice?"

"Whole wheat wins only if it's truly whole. Many 'wheat' breads still use refined flour. Read the ingredients — the first word should be 'whole.'"

"Marketing often hides refined flour behind 'wheat' labels."
